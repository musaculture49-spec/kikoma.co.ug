About Us

Kyankima Women’s Cooperative was founded in Mukono District by 47 women determined to improve livelihoods through agriculture, handcrafts, and health awareness. Our mission is to empower women and strengthen communities.

We began as a small group sharing skills and resources. Today, we are a registered community-based organization with growing market reach and impact.
